I have been selected to participate in the 13th [Heidelberg Laureate Forum](https://www.heidelberg-laureate-forum.org/about-us/) (HLF), held from September 13 to 18, 2026. It is a great opportunity to meet and exchange ideas with the laureates of the most prestigious awards in mathematics and computer science --- the Abel Prize, the ACM A.M. Turing Award, the ACM Prize in Computing, the Fields Medal, the IMU Abacus Medal, and the Nevanlinna Prize --- alongside fellow young researchers from around the world.
