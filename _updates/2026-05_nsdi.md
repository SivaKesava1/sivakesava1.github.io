We have two papers accepted at [NSDI 2026]! The first paper, =sc^Eywa^sc=, led by students Rajdeep Mondal and Rathin Singha, uses LLMs to automatically build rich models of protocol behavior from RFCs and other natural language sources for model-based testing, discovering 33 unique bugs across DNS, BGP, and SMTP implementations. The second paper, =sc^MetaEase^sc=, led by student Pantea Karimi, introduces a practical analyzer that identifies worst-case heuristic performance directly from source code, eliminating the need for formal modeling.
